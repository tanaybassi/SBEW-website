About SBEW

Built on precision.
Trusted by OEMs.

Shree Balaji Engineering Works has been delivering precision-machined components and fabricated assemblies since 2007. From our modern facility in Hoshiarpur, Punjab, we supply automotive Tier-1s, gearbox manufacturers, and export customers across Canada and Europe.

We operate a fully equipped machine shop with CNC turning centres, VMC machining, and a dedicated inspection room — backed by an ISO 9001:2015 quality system and Mitutoyo measurement instruments.

Quality System

Zero-defect mindset.
ISO-certified process.

01

Material Verification

Every batch begins with material test report (MTR) verification against chemical composition and mechanical property requirements.

02

In-Process Inspection

Dimensional checks at each machining stage. Critical features verified with Mitutoyo instruments per the approved control plan.

03

Final Dimensional Verification

100% dimensional check on first articles. Statistical sampling per AQL on production batches. All records retained.

04

Batch Traceability

Heat number, lot identification, and inspection records maintained per component batch. Full traceability from raw material to despatch.

05

Packaging & Preservation

VCI poly packaging, OEM-specified crating, and ocean-freight packaging for export consignments. Parts delivered ready-to-use.
